Cookie Preparation

"A Guide on How to Eat your Cookies"

  • Try to serve the cookies as soon as it is delivered and received specially for chocolate chip cookies.
  • Eat the chocolate as it is still sticky and melting on your mouth as you take a bite from it.
  • Place the cookies in an airtight container or a room temperature area in a dry place to keep its consistency an potency of its taste.
  • It is not recommended to store them in the refrigerator if it is not inside an airtight container as it dries faster.
  • If you are not able to finish the serving of cookies you ordered you can wrap it in foil and store it in the freezer.
  • When heating the cookies you can turn the heat up to 175 Celsius for 2-6 minutes for guaranteed fresh cookies.
  • You can store the cookies up to 1 month and then reheat before serving again.
  • Frozen cookies needs to be heated up to 175 Celsius for 3-8 minutes then let it thaw for 2 minutes before serving.
